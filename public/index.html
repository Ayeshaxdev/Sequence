How to Play

Objective: Be the first team to complete the required number of Sequences — 5 chips in a row.

On Your Turn

  • Play a card from your hand to claim the matching space on the board.
  • Place one of your team's chips on that space.
  • Draw a replacement card from the deck.

Jack Special Rules

  • One-eyed Jacks (J♠, J♥) — Remove any opponent chip from the board (not from a completed sequence).
  • Two-eyed Jacks (J♦, J♣) — Wild! Place your chip on any empty space.

Sequences

5 chips in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). Corner spaces are free for all teams. Chips in a completed sequence cannot be removed.

Win Conditions

  • First Sequence: First team to complete 1 sequence wins.
  • Two Sequences: First team to complete 2 sequences wins.
  • Play All Cards: First player to empty their hand wins.

Teams

Max 2 players per team. You need at least 2 players (1 per team) to start. Players without a team are not dealt cards.

Strategy Tips

  • 🎯 Focus on building your sequence while blocking opponents.
  • 🃏 Save Two-eyed Jacks for critical spots — they're powerful wildcards.
  • 🚫 Use One-eyed Jacks to disrupt opponent sequences before they complete.
  • ⭐ Corner spaces are free — route your sequences through them.
  • 🤝 In team mode, coordinate with your teammate to build sequences together.
  • 🔄 Cards that have all board positions occupied are "dead cards" — the host can declare a dead hand to allow a discard.
