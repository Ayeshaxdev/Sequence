How to Play

Objective

Form sequences — 5 chips in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). Team with the most sequences when no moves remain wins.

Your Turn

  1. Select a card from your hand.
  2. Place your chip on a matching board slot.
  3. A new card is drawn automatically.

Special Cards

Two-Eyed Jack — wild card, place your chip on any empty slot.

One-Eyed Jack — remove any opponent's chip from the board.

Dead Cards & Discard

A card is dead when both matching board slots are already occupied. On your turn, you may discard one dead card to draw a replacement, then play your normal turn. Only 1 discard per turn.

Corners

The 4 corner slots count as occupied for every team and can complete any sequence.

Intersections

Sequences can share chips, but two sequences may share at most one chip. A single chip can be part of multiple sequences in different directions.

Setup

  • Players: 2–6 (teams of 2 or 3)
  • Hand: 7 cards each
  • Board: 10x10 grid of playing cards
