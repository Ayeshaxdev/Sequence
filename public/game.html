How to Play

Objective

Form sequences — 5 chips in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). Team with the most sequences when no moves remain wins.

Special Cards

Two-Eyed Jack — wild card, place your chip on any empty slot.

One-Eyed Jack — remove any opponent's chip from the board.
